Optional: Download your X archive

Downloading your archive gives you a personal backup before deleting anything. This is optional, but recommended if you’re anxious about losing history.

  1. Go to Settings and privacy
  2. Select Your account
  3. Choose Download an archive of your data
  4. Confirm your password and request the archive
  5. X will email you when it’s ready (can take hours or days)

Step 1 — 10-Minute Safety Lockdown

Goal: stop new damage before touching old posts.

Action How to do it (official X help)
Protect your posts (recommended for safety) Protect posts
Disable discoverability by email/phone Discoverability settings
Remove location clues from profile Edit profile
Revoke unknown connected apps Connected apps
Enable two-factor authentication Set up 2FA
Why isn’t this automatic?

Automatic scanning is less accurate, less safe, and more likely to backfire. This tool avoids account access on purpose.

  • AI can’t reliably judge personal context
  • Bulk automation can trigger account restrictions
  • Most real damage comes from a small number of posts

Step 2 — The Top-25 Risk Purge

This step is guided — not automated. You identify the posts. The method keeps you focused.

How to find your Top-25

  • Start with pinned and high-engagement posts
  • Search your profile for conflicts and personal details
  • Apply the risk test below
  • Stop at 25 — more is not better

Risk test (any one = high risk)

  • Reveals location, routine, health, family, or money
  • Looks bad without context
  • Shows anger, insults, or threats
  • Would hurt you if screenshot by someone hostile
